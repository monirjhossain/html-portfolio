BE KIND TO YOUR SKIN

Our rose water is made from rose petals with no added extra fragrances. It is a hydrating formula that helps restore moisture, making the skin look fresh. Rose water helps to get the dirt out of the pores and helps maintain the skin’s pH balance. This body toner has an active cleansing agent that penetrates deep into pores to cleanse them, locking in hydration to increase elasticity. Aqua, Allantoin, Distilled Rose Water, PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil, Rose Oil, Phenoxyethanol, DMDM Hydantoin, Vitamin C.
